About

About

I'm a Dutch composer, producer and sound designer with a deep passion for trailer music, game audio and cinematic scores. My journey began at the Prins Claus Conservatory, where I honed my style in composition and studio production. As a former DJ and producer (Noisecontrollers), I connected with global audiences through collaborations like Get Loose with Showtek and Tiësto.

Today I write across genres for the world's biggest games, trailers and brands, from World of Warcraft and Destiny 2 to BMW, blending classical, contemporary and electronic into work that's emotional, precise and larger than life. Call it a wall of sound.
